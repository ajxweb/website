There I was, lonely again, being my sissy self. I was sitting on a big rock in some park, just bored and wearing my cute school cosplay uniform.
At least my pale made it outside the house, I thought to myself.

"Hey cutie," I heard a voice say. Confused, I looked around, brushing my vivid, black hair out of my bored face.
"Who are you..?" I managed to say. The stupid fucking wind kept blowing my hair back into my face.

Halfway in the shade was a tall, a girl with brown hair. she had on a Chemical Romance tshirt and looked like she could kill me .

"I'm Y ," she stated confidently as she sat next to me on the rock. I smiled a little, still confused.

"On second thought," i was nervous, "let's just stay here ."
